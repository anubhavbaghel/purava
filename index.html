Our Legacy

Hygiene Redefined.

For over a decade, Purava has been at the forefront of bathroom innovation, merging engineering precision with timeless luxury to create a standard of purity trusted across the nation.

The Purava Journey

A Commitment to Excellence from Day One

Founded with a singular vision to elevate bathroom hygiene, Purava started as a passionate endeavor to create the perfect health faucet. We realized that while luxury was often prioritized in bathroom design, true hygiene equipment was overlooked.

Today, with more than 10 years of experience, we have transformed into a leading manufacturer of premium bathroom fixtures. Our journey is defined by a relentless pursuit of quality and a deep understanding of the sophisticated needs of modern Indian homes.

We don't just manufacture products; we engineer sanctuaries. Every Purava fixture is a testament to our dedication to your health and the elegance of your living spaces.
